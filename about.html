Profile

  • Researcher with eight years of experience in different projects at the intersection of Humanities and Information Technologies, with a focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP) Applications.
  • Academic background in Philology and Medieval Studies with a strong foundation in Romance Linguistics and Latin.
  • Strong programming skills.
  • Expertise in digital editing and the creation of interfaces for philological textual analysis aided by NLP tools.
  • Participation in international and interdisciplinary teams; elected member of the Text Encoding Initiative’s Technical Council (TEI-C) since January 2021.
  • Active involvement in dissemination and training activities related to Digital Humanities.
  • Student supervision experience at postgraduate level (MA and PhD).
  • Experience in group management and coordination of research activities.
  • Day-to-day involvement in collaborative works through which I have developed good interpersonal and teamwork skills.
