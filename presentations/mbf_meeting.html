Project selection

Digital workstation for the analysis of linguistic variation across witnesses

Goals Methods
Gain new knowledge about the Galician-Portuguese lyric manuscript tradition from the systematic analysis of linguistic variation phenomena between witnesses
  • Data modelling and standardisation: from DOCX to a TEI-XML edition
  • Automatic collation
  • Interface development (interface as part of the philological discourse)
  • Statistical analysis
https://github.com/HelenaSabel/variatio_delectat

POSTDATA: A network of ontologies for European poetry

POSTDATA Project

Goals Methods
Creation of the means to publish analytical annotations related to any European Poetry tradition as Linked Open Data
  • Reverse engineering (from logical models to conceptual models)
  • Data modelling (from individual conceptual models to a common data model)
  • Ontology engineering

Anathomie by Gui de Chauliac

Anathomie by Gui de Chauliac

Goals Methods
  • Creation of an interactive digital edition
  • Certain words of the edition are connected to the corresponding lemma in the Dictionnaire Étymologique de l’Ancien Français (DEAF)
  • Data modelling (from LaTeX to TEI-XML)
  • Semantic web technologies (RDFa)

DIachronic Spanish sonnet COrpus and DISCOver

DIachronic Spanish sonnet COrpus and DISCOver

Goals Methods
  • Inclusion of non-canonical authors
  • Provide rich metadata and literary annotations (obtained automatically)
  • Enable the navigation of the corpus and the annotations through an interface
  • Data collection (web scraping)
  • Digital editing
  • Automatic annotation of: enjambment, rhyme schemes and scansion
  • Data visualisation
https://github.com/pruizf/disco + https://github.com/HelenaSabel/DISCOver

Édition hyperDIplomatique de la lyrique GAlégo-portugaise (DIGA)

DIGA Project

Goals Methods
Design of a digital edition model that enables the explotation of any paleographic or codicological phenomenon
  • Data modelling (including presentational information)
  • Interface development

CAREXIL-FR: Letters by Spanish Republicans Refugees and Exiles in France

CAREXIL-FR Project

Goal Methods
  • Digital edition of the letters written by Spanish refugees in French camps after the end of the Spanish Civil War
  • Sociolinguistic and historical analysis of these letters
  • Data modelling
  • Automatic linguistic annotation
  • Manual linguistic annotation (TEITOK)
  • Statistical analysis

WoPoss: A World of Possibilities

Modal pathways over an extra-long period of time: the diachrony of modality in the Latin language

Goal Methods
Reconstruction of the evolution of modal meanings (possibility, necessity and volition) from the prehistory of the Latin language to the 7th century
  • Data collection
  • Automatic linguistic annotation
  • Manual semantic annotation (INCEpTION)
  • Conversion between standards
  • Statistical analysis
https://github.com/WoPoss-project

Goal Methods
